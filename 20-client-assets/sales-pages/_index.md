# Sales pages

Markdown copies of the client's current landing pages, sales pages, and offer
pages. The auto-generated `scraped-pages.md` in the research bundle has summaries
of off-platform pages linked from videos, but those are scraped quickly — the
files here are the canonical full-text versions.

## Conventions

- One file per page. Filename: `<page-slug>.md` matching the URL slug.
- Each file: URL, what the page sells, the full body text, any internal notes
  (this offer is paused / this was last updated when / etc.)
