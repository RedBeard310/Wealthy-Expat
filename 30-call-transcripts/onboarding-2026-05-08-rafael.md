## Casey's content-type hierarchy ("closest to the money")

In order, best to worst for booking sales calls:

1. **Client case studies / narrative transformations** — real client, real result, ideally with photo/interview/evidence. Hardest to produce given Rafael's privacy-sensitive ICP, but highest converting.
2. **Deep-dive diagnosis** — "why X is over," "why Y is a trap," "why you think X but it's actually Y."
3. **Frameworks / methodologies** — Rafael's named systems for choosing passports, protecting wealth, etc.
4. **Cost & pricing breakdowns** — what working with Rafael actually costs; what a passport really costs beyond the headline donation figure.
5. **Hardcore educational / how-to** — step-by-step tutorials.

Casey's stance: views are vanity. "My goal for you would be fewer views than you've ever gotten and more money than you ever got." Viral and BoFu rarely overlap; pick one, and BoFu wins for this channel's goal.
