## Channel About

The Dominica Citizenship by Investment Unit (CBIU) is the official government authority that administers the Dominica Citizenship by Investment Programme. 

Dominica’s Citizenship by Investment Programme, headed by Marie-Therese Johnson, is one of the oldest programmes of its kind and the most trustworthy.

Introduced in 1993 as a means of driving much-needed foreign direct investment to the island, it allows highly reputable individuals and their extended families to diversify their investment portfolio and gain second citizenship of a country that is welcoming and safe haven.

For more government insights, and information visit: https://cbiu.gov.dm/
